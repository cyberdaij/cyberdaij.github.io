Home

    My name is Jun Dai. I'm currently an Associate Professor in Department of Computer Science at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). My research interests mainly lie in network and distributed system security, AI and security, with focus on intrusion detection, vulnerability analysis, secure programming, and cybersecurity education.

    Recruitment: I'm looking for self-motivated students at all levels (undergraduates, master, and PhD). I have PhD positions with full RA/TA support. If you are interested, please email me. Post-doctoral and visiting researchers or students are also welcome.
