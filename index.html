Home

    Hello, welcome to my homepage. My name is Jun Dai. I'm an Associate Professor in Department of Computer Science at California State University, Sacramento (Sacramento State). I am also the Director of the Center for Information Assurance and Security (CIAS) at Sacramento State. My research interests mainly lie in Network and Distributed System Security, Intrusion Detection, and Secure Programming. My research is funded by NSF and NSA. I advise the Cyber Defense Club here, and coach student teams to play and win in cybersecurity competitions such as CCDC, CyberForce and Hivestorm.
